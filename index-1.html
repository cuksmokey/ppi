About
Guests loved walking around the neighbourhood!
Located only a 5-minute walk from Kuta Square, Zia Hotel Kuta offers a cosy and modern stay featuring an outdoor swimming pool, a pool bar, and an on-site restaurant. Guests can make use of the fitness centre. The property is 5 minutes' walk to Kuta Beach, traditional market, and shopping centre. Legian's nightlife is 10 minutes' walk away. Bali Denpasar International Airport is about 6 km away. Each room at Zia Hotel Kuta is fitted with air conditioning, seating area, minibar, electric kettle, and flat-screen cable TV with USB plug. There is an en suite bathroom featuring shower facility with tempered glass.
